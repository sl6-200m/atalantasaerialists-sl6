About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Atlanta Aerialists has become the premier aerial arts company in the Southeast. Our team of professional performers brings years of experience from circus arts, dance, and theater to create breathtaking performances.

We specialize in aerial silks, lyra (aerial hoop), trapeze, and unique apparatus combinations that push the boundaries of aerial performance.
